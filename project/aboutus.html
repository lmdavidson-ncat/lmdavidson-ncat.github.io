Our History:

Lilly B was created in honor of our CEO's grandmother, Lillian Beatrice. Miss Beatrice was a force to be reckoned with in the education world and was an excellent coder. Unfortuantely, Miss Beatrice passed in 2022. This game was one way to keep her memory alive while honoring her with a way to teach others.


Our Mission:

Our mission is to make learning HTML fun and engaging by combining it with a typing game that improves both your coding and typing skills. We believe in interactive learning that helps users grasp the fundamentals of web development in a hands-on way!


Our Team:

Our team consists of web developers and educators passionate about teaching HTML, CSS, and JavaScript. We created this game to help you become confident in your coding abilities.
